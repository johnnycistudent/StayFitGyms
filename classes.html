Classes

Classes

Your health is your one of the most important assests in life. At at StayFit we understand that this and have assembled a great range of classes to keep you healthy fit and full of life. Whether your fitness goals are to lose weight, tone up, improve endurance levels, gain muscle or strength and we have a host of different classes to achieve your targets in a motivating environment and with instructors to give your a helping hand. Nearly all of our classes are included in your membership fee at no extra cost, unless stated otherwise. A full list of the classes we offer are listed below. To make finding the right classes for you even more simple, we have grouped our classes into the categories below:

kettlebell

Your kettlebell classes are fun, efficient and energising. StayFit offers Kettlebells classes with classes ranging from 25-45 minutes. Don’t worry if you’re new to kettlebell training. The instructors are very helpful and will guide you though getting started. The weights vary so you can pick what’s best for you and our instructors will guide you through step-by-step.

Yoga

This invigorating candlelit class provides the perfect balance between body movement and meditation. This class is suitable for all levels. StayFit's Yoga class brings a holistic approach to health, fitnessness and life. Yoga will tone up your body, clear your mind and improve your overall well-being.

Strength Training

Strength training may involve the combining of different training methods such as weight training, plyometrics, bodyweight exercises, and ballistic exercises. StayFit's classes are working towards improving your ability to apply your strength quickly with the end goal to increase ones explosive power to the maximum potential.

Swimming

You are never too young or old to start your amazing journey towards confidence in the water. Where better to learn than StayFit's swimming centre? We provide a glowing welcome and ongoing help and support for anyone who wants to learn to swim, with classes for kids, and adults alike.

Pilates

Pilates is a challenging, low impact, total body workout that focuses on core strength and elongating the body. The Pilates Reformer is a resistance based machine that uses springs to strengthen and stretch, allowing the body to develop into a long, strong, lean form. Our classes are available for beginners and advanced gym goers alike.

Boxing

Boxing is great for getting the heart rate up and the body moving and the sweat pouring. Throughout your training you’ll going to learn beginner, intermediate and advanced boxing techniques combined with high intensity circuits to increase your cardio fitness. Workouts will combine different boxing combinations with different exercises. You will finish each workout with a traditional boxing core workout.

Let The Good Times Roll

We’re big believers in fitness being fun and that’s an integral as part of our classes. Join us and pop in to try one for yourself. Sign up to our mailing list to find out up and coming news about our classes!
