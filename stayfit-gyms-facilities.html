Facilities

StayFit facilities include state of the art equipment to sculpt your body and achieve your health goals. StayFit provides its members a state-of-the-art gymnasium, that are managed by our expert team of experienced and qualified fitness instructors.

The facilities include a full sized olympic swimming pool with a steam room, jacuzzi and sauna. Next to the Swimming complex Where we offer a Stayfit's gym main hall. We have a vast range of cardio equipment treadmills, assault bikes, rowing machines, spin bikes to cater for all the needs of our members and to help them achieve their exercise goals. StayFit's gym also hosts a wide range of new weight plates and dumbells as well as now having the latest weight machines, benches and racks to help you build muscle and stay lean.
